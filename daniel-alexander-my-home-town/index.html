Top three activities to do in Enfield

Explore Trent Park

Trent Park offers vast woodlands, meadows, and lakes. Perfect for a day out, you can enjoy nature walks, picnics, or visit the Wildlife Rescue Centre. It even has a Go Ape!

Train with Tai Tsung Martial Arts Club

Join Tai Tsung Kung Fu Club at Southbury Leisure Centre for Sanda and classical Kung Fu classes. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, the classes cater to all ages and levels and provide a great way to stay fit and disciplined.

Support the local team

Cheer for Enfield's local football team at Enfield Town FC. Get a real feel for community spirit by supporting the country's first fan-owned club at their home ground, Queen Elizabeth II Stadium.
